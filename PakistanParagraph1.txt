pakistan officially the islamic republic of pakistan is a country in south asia it is the world fifth-most populous country
 with a population exceeding 212.2 million it has the world's second-largest muslim population it is the 33rd-largest 
country by area, spanning 881,913 square kilometres (340,509 square miles) pakistan has a 1,046-kilometre (650-mile) 
coastline along the arabian sea and gulf of oman in the south and is bordered by india to the east, afghanistan to the 
west, iran to the southwest, and china to the northeast it is separated narrowly from tajikistan by afghanistan wakhan 
corridor in the northwest and also shares a maritime border with oman
